Close Protection Operative

The best training for bodyguards and professional military personnel on 3 continents

Enroll course

  • Duration

    • 6 days

  • Weapons

    • Pistols – Glock 17
    • Glock 19
    • Sig Sauer
    • Beretta 92F

  • Gear

    • Casual clothing (no jeans), rain / weather gear and business clothing (slacks, jacket, tie and appropriate shoes), tactical cargo pants, tactical belt (sturdy) , sport shoes, sport uniform

  • Skill Prerequisites

    • None. The candidate does not have to possess prior experiance in Close Protection.

  • Whats Includes

    • Accommodation, 3 meals per day, weapons, ammunition, transfer from/to Airport, EBSSA international certificate, etc.

About course

The Close Protection Operative course will teach you how to manage security of a VIP, organize a security team and all the logistics of prevention.

Protection work is very demanding and exhausting. For that reason it is important to have an excellent preparation. To ensure you don’t make mistakes in the future, our staff with different jobs in the sector will pass on all their experience.
